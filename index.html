How to shoot
  • One item per photo — fill the frame
  • Plain background: concrete, wood, or a dark surface
  • Vary the angle: top-down, slight tilt, on its side
  • Vary sizes within each class (M3 through M12 etc.)
  • Even lighting — avoid harsh glare or deep shadows
  • Keep the phone still to avoid motion blur
